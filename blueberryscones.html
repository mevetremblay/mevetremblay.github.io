Lemon Blueberry Scones



Double this recipe and make sure you to keep a stash of these homemade morsels in the freezer to serve to visitors who drop in unexpectedly.


Prep: 15 minutes


Bake: 20 minutes


Serves: 12 scones

    Ingredients for scones

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter, cold, grated
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 lemon zest finely grated
  • 1 cup heavy cream, plus more for brushing

    • Ingredients for glaze

  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

DIRECTIONS FOR SCONES

Preheat the oven to 400℉ degrees.

Combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar and gently whisk together. Add the grated cold butter to the dry mixture and toss to combine so the butter is evenly distributed.Fold the blueberries and lemon zest into the dry mixture.

Make a well in the center and pour in the heavy cream. Fold everything together just to incorporate; do not overwork the dough.Form 12 medium sized mounds out of the dough.

Place on a parchment lined baking sheet and bake in the pre-heated oven for 16-20 minutes, or until the top just barely turned golden brown.


DIRECTIONS FOR GLAZE

Combine the lemon juice, powdered sugar and melted butter in a small bowl and whisk until no clumps remain. Once the scones have cooled, drizzle with the glaze and let cool.
